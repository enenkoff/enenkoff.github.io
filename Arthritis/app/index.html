Creating hope in Australians with arthritis

No matter your walk of life, or experience with arthritis, we’re here to help you manage your symptoms and get on with life.

Find out more
  • learn more about arthritis
  • managing my arthritis
  • get support for your arthritis
  • get involved and help others

Nearly one in five Australians,
that's 3.85 million people - have arthritis.

Arthritis Australia is a charitable not-for-profit organisation, and is the peak arthritis body in Australia. It is supported by affiliate offices in ACT, New South Wales, Northern Territory, Queensland, South Australia, Tasmania and Western Australia.
